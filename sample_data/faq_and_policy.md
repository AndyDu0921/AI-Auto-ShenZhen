# FAQ and Policy

## Sampling
- Sample lead time: 3-5 working days if stock is available.
- Sample fee can be refunded in the first bulk order when order quantity exceeds 1000 pcs.

## Trade terms
- Common quotation terms: EXW Shenzhen, FOB Shenzhen.

- Spare parts can be included in bulk orders upon request.
